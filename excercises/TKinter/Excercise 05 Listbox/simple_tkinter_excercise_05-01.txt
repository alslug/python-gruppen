Title: Simple TKinter Class 05-01
Simple Tkinter class with Label, listbox, entry, buttons, functions and methods.
This project shows a label with the text "Wish List:", a listbox with a predefined list ["Winnie Poe", "Guitar", "Hot Wheels Track", "Camera"], a entry containing the text "Nintendo Switch" and  two button with the text "Add a wish" and "Delete a Wish"
